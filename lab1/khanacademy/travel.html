Travel to Finland

3 reasons why you should travel to Finland

  1. Nature & Scenery: Lapland's Arctic wilderness, over 180,000 lakes, vast forests, and the Northern Lights.
  2. Sauna Culture: With over 3 million saunas for 5.5 million people, it is a deeply rooted tradition.
  3. Winter Wonderland: Santa Claus Village in Rovaniemi (Arctic Circle), husky/reindeer sledding, and glass igloos.

Finland is globally renowned as the "happiest country in the world,"famous for its exceptional education system, extensive sauna culture (over 3 million), and pristine natural landscapes, including Lapland and thousands of lakes. It is a top destination for viewing the Northern Lights, visiting Santa Claus in Rovaniemi, and experiencing unique winter activities like ice swimming and husky sledding.
